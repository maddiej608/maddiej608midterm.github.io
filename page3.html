Why we dance.
Dance is so much more than simply moving your body and spending extensive amounts of time at the studio. It's even more than just a sport, too. Dancers enjoy their craft for so many different reasons. These reasons will always be more significant than the struggles.



Dance isn't only a sport. It's an art and it's own language. It takes skills in acting and performance, but also endurance and strength. Here's some things that dancers have to say about the art:



"Dance is the hidden language of the soul." Martha Graham
“Dancing is my life force, my passion, and my way of expressing myself.” Maria Tallcheif
“The only excuse for dancing is grace and beauty of movement.” Anna Pavlova
